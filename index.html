About PopIn

PopIn is your go-to app for real-time, location-based networking. Whether you're at a professional event, a bar, or a casual gathering, PopIn helps you discover and connect with people around you.

Features

  • Real-time connection to people at the same location
  • Easy profile setup: Share who you are, what you need, and what you offer
  • In-app messaging to communicate with new connections
  • Special features for event organizers to manage events and engage attendees
